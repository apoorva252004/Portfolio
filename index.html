About Me

I'm a Data Science undergraduate at RV University, Bengaluru, pursuing a minor in Economics. Currently interning at the IISc Sustainability Research Lab (since Jan 2026), where I apply data-driven approaches to sustainability challenges.

My work sits at the crossroads of machine learning, econometrics, and public policy. I care deeply about building models that are not just accurate — but interpretable, fair, and meaningful.

From explainable AI for education policy to a patent-winning geographic-indication game, I'm drawn to projects where data meets real-world purpose. Outside of data, I find grounding in classical dance, Indian music, and photography.
